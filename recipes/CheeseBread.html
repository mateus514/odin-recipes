Cheese Bread

Fast and Delicious Recipe!

Description:

This is a delicious cheese bread recipe with only 3 ingredients, perfect to enjoy with a cup of coffee, and perfect for many other occasions.

Quick and delicious for those who don't feel like following many long steps.

Let's get started with the delicious recipe!

Ingredients:

  • 200ml of Cream Milk
  • 1 cup (cream cheese container) of grated cheese
  • 1 cup (cream cheese container) of tapioca starch

Steps:

First, added 600g of Tapioca starch, 200ml of Cream Milk and 200g of grated Cheese in a recipe, and mix well until a dought forms

Then, keep mix well until it starts to come away from your hands.

Now form the dough into small balls and bake them at 180 degrees.

Wait for it to cool down. Now its Ready to serve!
