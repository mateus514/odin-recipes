Brigadeiro

Famous Brazzilian Candy

Description:

Brigadeiro, also known as the Brazilian chocolate truffle, is a famous and beloved sweet in Brazil. It is aprecious on many occasions such as parties, movie nights, and desserts, and is known for its rich chocolate flavor and creamy texture.

This simple and delicious recipe uses only a few ingredients and takes just minutes to make. It is perfect for those who love the combination of rich chocolate and creamy texture. Brigadeiro can be served with chocolate sprinkles, in paper cups, or in any creative way you prefer.

Let's get started with the delicious recipe!

Ingredients:

  • 1 can of sweetened condensed milk
  • 1 can of Cream Milk
  • 2 tablespoos of chocolate powder
  • 1 tablespoon of butter
  • Chocolate Sprinkles (optional)

Steps:

  1. First, add one tablespoon of butter to a saucepan and place it over low heat.
  2. Then, add the sweetened condensed milk, the heavy cream, and two tablespoons of chocolate powder. Stir well until fully combined.
  3. When it starts to pull away from the bottom of the pan, turn off the heat. Its a ready!
  4. Now, if you prefer, let the brigadeiro cool. Then, lightly grease your hands with butter, roll it into small balls, coat them with chocolate sprinkles , place them in paper cups, and serve. (optional)
