Mousse of powdered drink mix

Fast and Delicious Recipe

Description:

This recipe is for a delicious and creamy dessert: the famous and beloved mousse, perfect for any occasion.

It requires only three ingredients and delivers an amazing flavor!

Let's get started with the delicious recipe!

Ingredients:

  • 1 can of sweetened condensed milk
  • 1 can of Creamy Milk
  • 1 Powdered Drink Mix

Steps:

  1. First, put 1 can of sweetened condensed milk, 1 can of heavy cream, and 1 Powdered Drink Mix in a blender, and blend well for 2 minutes.
  2. Then, turn it on and let it blend for 2 minutes, until mixed.
  3. Now, pour it into a mold of your choice and freeze for 2 hours. Now it's ready to serve!
